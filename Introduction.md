# How To Pass Your Security+ Exam
*Certification requirements & best methods for studying the Security+ Exam*  
  
SY0-701 is a specific security+ exam. You need to verify that this is the current version of the exam. If there is a newer one, then you need to find the most updated version of the course.  
  
The course comes with many more videos and knowledge to learn. The one's from professor messer are just a start.  
  
**You can find all for free on youtube**  
  
### Exam Details:
- Single Exam
- 90 minutes long
- Scale from 100-900 (need 750)

### Common Exam Areas
- 1.0 General Security Concepts (12%)
- 2.0 Threats, Vulnerabilities, and Mitigations (22%)
- 3.0 Security Architecture (18%)
- 4.0 Security Operations (28%)
- 5.0 Security Program Management and Oversight (20%)
  
Majority consist of multiple choice questions with single answers. However they also ask other types of questions which are usually at the beginning. Matching, Drag and Drop, or sorting. (Performance Based Questions)  
  
### TO STUDY
**Grab the CompTIA Security+ certification objectives and learn them side by side with the videos available.**
  
Also important to read and get hands on.  
  
You can take the exam online or in person. The online testing usually requires a bit more prep so that's the downside to doing it so they make sure you aren't cheating.
